Pilsner • Lager

Audience
Early risers, fishing days, boat people, and anyone chasing a cold, easy first beer.

Our story

Born in the outdoors. Rooted in Weslaco.

lowcase Brewing blends Pacific Northwest exploration with South Texas hospitality—trail days, harbor mornings, backyard fogatas, and slow nights with good company.

The name

lowcase is an anagram of Weslaco.

A nod to the place and pace behind the beer—clever, local, and easygoing.

The vibe

From trailheads to backyard hangs.

It’s a ride before sunset, a cast at first light, and a fire after dark. The beers are built for those moments.

The beer

Every beer is a moment.

From El Pescador at sunrise to Barrel Bandit after hours, each label captures a mood and a reason to reach for it.
